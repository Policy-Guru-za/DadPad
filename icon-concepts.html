PolishPad Icon Concepts

Five distinctive directions for the macOS application icon. Each is designed at 1024×1024 with the standard squircle mask.

Option 1
Faceted Gem

A precision-cut gemstone on deep navy. Raw text goes in, polished brilliance comes out.

Option 2
Quill Nib

A refined fountain pen nib with a drop of blue ink. The classic tool of precision writing.

Option 3
Luminous P

A bold "P" monogram with a diagonal polish-streak of light. The brand mark itself gets polished.

Option 4
The Prism

Raw text enters, refined tones emerge. A prism refracting a beam into a blue spectrum.

Option 5
Ensō Circle

A brushstroke circle of mastery with a blue polished core. Zen refinement through practice.
